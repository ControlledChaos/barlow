Barlow is a variation font superfamily available in 54 pre-defined styles. The typeface is named after internet pioneer, EFF co-founder, songwriter, and activist John Perry Barlow. Drawing from the visual style of the California polis &emdash; public signs along its highways; on its trains, busses, and cars &emdash; Barlow is a tribute to JBP's utopian vision for the information superhighway.
